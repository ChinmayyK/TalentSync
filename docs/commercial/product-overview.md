# TalentSync Product Overview

## What is TalentSync?

**TalentSync** is a multi-tenant Interview Management System (IMS) designed for internal recruiting teams to schedule, coordinate, and track candidate interviews at scale.

---

## What TalentSync IS

- **Interview scheduling platform** for internal recruiters and hiring managers
- **Candidate pipeline tracker** with stage-based progression
- **Multi-channel communication hub** (Email, SMS, WhatsApp)
- **Integration-ready** with major CRM and ATS systems
- **Multi-tenant SaaS** with enterprise security controls
- **Calendar-aware** with Google Calendar and Outlook integration

---

## What TalentSync is NOT

- ❌ Not an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) replacement
- ❌ Not a job posting or career site platform
- ❌ Not a background check or assessment tool
- ❌ Not a video interview platform
- ❌ Not an employee onboarding system

---

## Target Customer Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Company Size** | 50-5000 employees |
| **Hiring Volume** | 20+ interviews/month |
| **Team Size** | 2-50 recruiters |
| **Tech Maturity** | Uses cloud tools, has existing ATS |
| **Pain Point** | Interview scheduling chaos, poor candidate experience |

---

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Eliminate Scheduling Chaos
Centralized interview calendar with conflict detection, room booking, and interviewer availability.

### 2. Improve Candidate Experience
Automated reminders, easy rescheduling, multi-channel notifications.

### 3. Unify Recruiter Workflow
Single dashboard for candidates, interviews, feedback, and communication.

### 4. Integrate with Existing Tools
Two-way sync with CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho) and ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday).

### 5. Enterprise-Ready Security
SSO, RBAC, audit logs, encryption, SOC 2 aligned practices.

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- SSO via SAML 2.0 and OIDC
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
